I am a Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Ohio State University, where I lead the Architecture Research Lab. Our research focuses on computer architecture, energy-efficient computing, security, and reliability.

I received my Ph.D. from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I have received numerous awards, including the Ohio State CSE Department Teaching Award (2021), Lumley Award (2014), and NSF CAREER Award (2012).

I am thrilled to be part of the ACE Center for Evolvable Computing, where I lead the Security, Privacy, and Correctness theme.
